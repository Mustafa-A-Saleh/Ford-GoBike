What is the structure of your dataset?¶

After adjusting the dataset, we have 10 columns(duration_min, start_time, end_time, start_station_name, end_station_name, user_type, member_birth_year, member_gender, bike_share_for_all_trip, age) with 174952 observations.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

We need to focus on number of trips/users and duration.

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

I think duration and number of trips/users are affected by age, gender, day of the week and user type.
